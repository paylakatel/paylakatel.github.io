Thanks for stopping by! My name is Kayla and I am a recent graduate of Clark University with my M.S. in Geographic Information Science. My master's research focused on the spatial interactions of large and small-scale mining in Ghana, and the work was recently accepted to Extractive Industries and Society as an original research article.

I'm currently the Webmaster/GIS Technician for the Town of Acton, MA, and over the summer I was a participant in the NASA DEVELOP program at the Langley Research Center. On this site you'll find examples of projects and work that I have completed. Feel free to send me an email with any questions or comments!
