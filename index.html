Welcome

Thanks for stopping by! My name is Kayla, and I currently work for the City of Boston as a Data and Performance Analyst on the Citywide Analytics team. I graduated from Clark University with my M.S. in Geographic Information Science in August 2015, and my master's research focused on the spatial interactions of large and small-scale mining in Ghana. The work was published in Extractive Industries and Society as an original research article in April 2016. On this site you'll find examples of projects and work that I have completed. Feel free to send me an email with questions or comments!
